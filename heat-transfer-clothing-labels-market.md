<p><p>The Heat Transfer Clothing Labels market comprises primarily two types: vinyl and digital print. Vinyl labels are created using a heat press method that applies layers of colored vinyl onto fabric, offering durability and vivid colors. In contrast, digital print labels use advanced printing technology to directly print designs onto special transfer paper, allowing for complex graphics and gradients. Both methods cater to diverse branding needs, balancing cost, customization, and application ease, making them popular choices in the apparel industry.</p></p>
