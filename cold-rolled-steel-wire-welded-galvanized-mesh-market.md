Flared channel decking features a tapered design that enhances stability and load distribution, making it suitable for lightweight structures. U-channel decking, characterized by its U-shaped cross-section, offers strength and support for heavier loads, often used in industrial settings. Both types provide durability and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for construction, reinforcing structures, and safety barriers in various environments.</p></p>
