<p><p>The cervical interbody devices market comprises several types, including pre-filled, corpectomy, and expandable devices. Pre-filled devices are designed with bone graft material already included, promoting fusion at the surgical site. Corpectomy devices are utilized in more extensive procedures, where a vertebral body is removed, requiring a supportive device to stabilize the spine. Expandable devices offer adjustable height and support, allowing for customization during implantation, enhancing stability and enabling optimal alignment in cervical spine surgeries.</p></p>
