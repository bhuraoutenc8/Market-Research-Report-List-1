<p><p>The Rapid Antigen and Antibody Detection Kits market encompasses two primary types: Rapid Antigen Tests and Rapid Antibody Tests. Rapid Antigen Tests detect specific proteins from a pathogen, providing quick results for active infections, often used in disease outbreaks. In contrast, Rapid Antibody Tests identify antibodies produced in response to an infection, indicating past exposure. Both tests are valuable for diagnosing infectious diseases swiftly, enhancing public health responses and allowing for timely interventions, especially in resource-limited settings.</p></p>
