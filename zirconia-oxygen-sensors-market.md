<p><p>Zirconia oxygen sensors are categorized primarily into three market types: thimble, planar, and others. Thimble sensors feature a cylindrical design that allows for direct measurement of oxygen levels in exhaust gases, commonly used in automotive applications. Planar sensors, with a flat design, provide faster response times and are suitable for various applications, including industrial and environmental monitoring. The "others" category encompasses specialized sensors tailored for unique applications, such as laboratory use or specific industrial processes, enhancing versatility in oxygen measurement technologies.</p></p>
