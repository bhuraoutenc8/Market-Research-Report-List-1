<p><ul><li>Point Sensor</li><li>Fiber Optic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arc Sensor market comprises two main types: Point Sensors and Fiber Optic Sensors. Point Sensors are localized devices that detect electrical arcs at specific locations, offering precise measurements and rapid response times. In contrast, Fiber Optic Sensors utilize flexible optical fibers to monitor arc events over larger areas, providing real-time data transmission and enhanced sensitivity. Both types play critical roles in enhancing safety and performance in electrical systems, leveraging advanced technology to mitigate risks associated with arc faults.</p></p>
