<p><strong>The Benchtop TDS Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop TDS meters are categorized based on the number of channels they offer for testing total dissolved solids in various solutions. Single-channel meters allow for the measurement of one sample at a time, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual-channel meters enable simultaneous testing of two samples, enhancing efficiency for comparative analysis. Multi-channel meters can assess multiple samples concurrently, making them ideal for laboratories with high throughput demands. Each type caters to different laboratory needs, balancing complexity and operational flexibility.</p></p>
