<p><strong>The Haemodialysis Concentrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bicarbonate Concentrates</li><li>Acid Concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The haemodialysis concentrates market is primarily divided into bicarbonate and acid concentrates. Bicarbonate concentrates supply sodium bicarbonate, which helps maintain acid-base balance during dialysis, supporting patient stability. In contrast, acid concentrates typically contain various acids, such as acetic acid, which balance electrolytes and assist in fluid removal during the treatment. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring effective dialysis sessions, improving patient outcomes, and are essential components of the haemodialysis process for kidney failure management.</p></p>
