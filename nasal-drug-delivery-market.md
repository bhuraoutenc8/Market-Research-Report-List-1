<p><p>The nasal drug delivery market is segmented into three main types: unit dose, multi-dose, and metered dose. Unit dose delivers a single, pre-measured amount of medication, ensuring precise dosing and reducing contamination risk. Multi-dose systems allow multiple doses from a single container, enhancing convenience for chronic treatments while maintaining sterility. Metered dose devices dispense a specific volume of medication with each use, providing controlled delivery and ease of administration, making them suitable for both acute and chronic conditions.</p></p>
