<p><p>The ETC (Electronic Toll Collection) system market encompasses various technologies for automated toll collection. RFID-based systems utilize radio frequency identification for seamless transactions. DSRC-based systems rely on Dedicated Short-Range Communications for real-time data transfer between vehicles and toll infrastructure. Video analytic-based systems use cameras and image processing to identify vehicles and process tolls. GNSS/GPS-based systems leverage satellite technology for location tracking and toll determination, offering a flexible and efficient approach to toll collection across diverse roadways.</p></p>
