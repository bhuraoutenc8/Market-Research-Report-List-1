<p><p>The PAD medical device market includes various product types designed for treating peripheral artery disease. Peripheral vascular stents support narrowed arteries, while peripheral transluminal angioplasty balloon catheters help widen these vessels. PTA guidewires facilitate catheter placement, and embolic protection devices prevent debris from traveling during procedures. Chronic total occlusion devices specifically address completely blocked arteries. Aortic stents support the aorta, while inferior vena cava filters prevent blood clots from traveling to the lungs. Additional devices address other therapeutic needs in PAD management.</p></p>
