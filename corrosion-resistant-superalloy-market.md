<p><p>The corrosion-resistant superalloy market comprises three primary types: iron-based, cobalt-based, and nickel-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys are known for their strength and cost-effectiveness, often used in industrial applications. Cobalt-based superalloys excel in high-temperature environments, offering excellent wear resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for aerospace and medical applications. Nickel-based superalloys are highly versatile, providing outstanding corrosion resistance and mechanical performance, primarily utilized in gas turbines, chemical processing, and power generation industries. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
