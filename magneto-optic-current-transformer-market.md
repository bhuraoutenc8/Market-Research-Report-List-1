<p><p>The Magneto Optic Current Transformer (MOCT) market is categorized into two main types: Fiber Type and Non-Fiber Type. Fiber Type MOCTs utilize optical fibers to detect and measure electrical currents, offering high sensitivity, immunity to electromagnetic interference, and suitability for long-distance applications. Non-Fiber Type MOCTs, on the other hand, employ traditional materials and technologies without optical fibers, generally being used in shorter-range applications. Both types play crucial roles in power systems, ensuring accurate current measurement and enhancing system reliability.</p></p>
