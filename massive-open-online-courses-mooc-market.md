<p><p>Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) are educational platforms that offer large-scale online learning opportunities, often for free or at a low cost. The MOOC market can be categorized into two main types: CMOOC (Connectivist MOOC) and XMOOC (Traditional MOOC). CMOOCs focus on networking and collaboration among learners, emphasizing peer interaction and knowledge sharing. In contrast, XMOOCs are more structured, resembling traditional courses with predefined content, assessments, and instructor-led lectures, emphasizing standardized learning outcomes and scalability.</p></p>
