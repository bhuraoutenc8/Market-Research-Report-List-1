<p><p>Acrylonitrile-Butadiene Rubber (NBR) market is categorized into three main types: block type, particles/crumb type, and powder type. Block type NBR features a solid form, ideal for applications requiring durability and strength. Particles or crumb type NBR consists of small granules, enhancing flexibility and usability in various compounds. Powder type NBR, characterized by its fine granulation, is primarily used in coatings and adhesives, providing excellent consistency and performance in diverse industrial applications. Each type serves distinct manufacturing and application needs.</p></p>
