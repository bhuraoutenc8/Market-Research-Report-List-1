<p><p>The Anti-sway System market is divided mainly into hydraulic and mechanical systems. Hydraulic anti-sway systems utilize fluid power to control and reduce swaying movements in cranes and lifting equipment, offering precision and responsiveness. In contrast, mechanical anti-sway systems employ physical components like counterweights and linkages to achieve stability and minimize sway. Both systems aim to enhance safety and efficiency in material handling, but their operational mechanisms and applications differ, catering to varied industry needs and preferences.</p></p>
