<p><strong>The Automotive Safety System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Safety System</li><li>Passive Safety System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive safety system market is divided into two main types: active safety systems and passive safety systems. Active safety systems are designed to prevent accidents before they occur, utilizing technologies such as adaptive cruise control, automatic emergency braking, and lane-keeping assistance. In contrast, passive safety systems focus on minimizing injury during an accident, employing features like airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both systems play critical roles in enhancing overall vehicle safety and protecting occupants.</p></p>
