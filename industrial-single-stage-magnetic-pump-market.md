<p><p>The Industrial Single-stage Magnetic Pump market is segmented into two primary types: Stationary Shaft and Rotating Shaft magnetic pumps. Stationary Shaft pumps utilize a fixed magnetic coupling that securely transfers torque from the motor to the impeller without any physical contact, minimizing wear and leakage. In contrast, Rotating Shaft pumps feature a rotating magnetic coupling that allows for increased flexibility and reduced maintenance due to fewer components. Both types are favored for their efficient, leak-free operation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
