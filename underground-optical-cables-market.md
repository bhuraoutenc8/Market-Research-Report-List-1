<p><strong>The Underground Optical Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Singlemode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground optical cables market primarily features two types: multimode fiber and singlemode fiber. Multimode fiber is designed for short-distance communication, using multiple light rays to transmit data, making it ideal for local area networks (LANs). In contrast, singlemode fiber facilitates long-distance communication by allowing only one light ray to pass through, resulting in higher data rates over extended distances. Both types are crucial for reliable data transmission in various applications, including telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
