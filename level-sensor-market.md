<p><strong>The Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Level Sensors</li><li>Noncontact Level Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The level sensor market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact level sensors. Contact level sensors directly interact with the material they measure, commonly using floats, capacitive, or resistive methods, making them ideal for liquids and bulk solids. Non-contact level sensors, on the other hand, utilize techniques such as ultrasonic, radar, or laser to gauge levels without making physical contact, making them suitable for hostile environments or when contamination risk is a concern. Each type serves specific applications based on measurement requirements.</p></p>
