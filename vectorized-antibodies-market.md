<p><p>The vectorized antibodies market is segmented into several types based on delivery methods for gene therapy and vaccine applications. Adenoassociated Virus (AAV) vectors efficiently deliver genetic material to target cells, ensuring stable gene expression. Electroporation uses electrical pulses to enhance cell membrane permeability for antibody entry. Lipid Nanoparticles (LNPs) encapsulate mRNA or DNA, facilitating cellular uptake and delivery. Additional categories include other innovative methods such as polymer-based carriers and viral vectors, enhancing versatility in therapeutic applications.</p></p>
