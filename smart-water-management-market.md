<p><strong>The Smart Water Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR Meters</li><li>AMI Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Water Management encompasses advanced metering technologies used to optimize water distribution and consumption. AMR (Automated Meter Reading) meters automate data collection, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in billing, while reducing manual labor. In contrast, AMI (Advanced Metering Infrastructure) meters offer two-way communication, enabling real-time monitoring, data analytics, and proactive leak detection. Together, these technologies facilitate better resource management, improve operational efficiency, and promote sustainability in water systems, ultimately leading to cost savings and enhanced service for consumers.</p></p>
