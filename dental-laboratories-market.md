<p><p>The dental laboratory market comprises various types of prosthetic devices, primarily bridges, crowns, and dentures. Dental bridges are utilized to replace missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth, restoring function and aesthetics. Crowns act as caps for damaged or decayed teeth, providing strength and protection while enhancing appearance. Dentures, either partial or complete, are removable appliances designed to replace missing teeth and surrounding tissue, offering improved chewing ability and facial structure support for patients. Collectively, these products play vital roles in dental restoration and rehabilitation.</p></p>
