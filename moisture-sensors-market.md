<p><p>The moisture sensors market includes various types, each utilizing different technologies. Halogen moisture sensors leverage the heating effect of halogen bulbs to measure moisture content accurately. Infrared moisture sensors utilize infrared light to detect moisture levels through absorption analysis. Microwave moisture sensors employ microwave radiation to gauge moisture by measuring the reflection and transmission characteristics of materials. Each type serves diverse applications across industries such as agriculture, food processing, and construction, providing reliable solutions for moisture measurement and control.</p></p>
