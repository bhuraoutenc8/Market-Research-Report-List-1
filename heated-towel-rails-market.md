<p><ul><li>Electric Heated Towel Rails</li><li>Hydronic Heated Towel Rails</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heated towel rails market primarily comprises two types: electric and hydronic heated towel rails. Electric heated towel rails operate independently through electrical connections, providing instant heat and easy installation. They are ideal for quick use in various spaces. In contrast, hydronic heated towel rails are connected to a home’s hot water system, offering consistent warmth and energy efficiency. Both types enhance comfort and luxury in bathrooms, with choices depending on user needs, installation preferences, and heating systems.</p></p>
