<p><ul><li>Polyurethane-based</li><li>Cyanoacrylate-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The General Surgery Tissue Adhesive market primarily consists of two types: polyurethane-based and cyanoacrylate-based adhesives. Polyurethane-based adhesives offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of surgical applications. They can adapt to tissue movements and provide long-lasting adhesion. In contrast, cyanoacrylate-based adhesives cure rapidly upon contact with moisture, creating strong bonds quickly. These are often used for wound closure and surgical applications where speed is critical. Both types enhance surgical outcomes and reduce complication rates.</p></p>
