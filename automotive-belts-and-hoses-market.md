<p><p>The automotive belts and hoses market is primarily divided into two main types: drive belts and timing belts. In contrast, timing belts synchronize the rotation of the crankshaft and camshaft, ensuring precise engine timing for optimal performance. Both types are crucial for vehicle operation, impacting efficiency and longevity, which drives demand in the automotive sector.</p></p>
