<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scanner market is categorized into three types based on slice capacity. Low-slice scanners, with fewer than 64 slices, are typically used for basic imaging needs. Medium-slice scanners, specifically 64-slice systems, offer enhanced resolution and faster imaging, making them suitable for a range of diagnostic applications. High-slice scanners, exceeding 64 slices, provide the highest resolution and speed, allowing for advanced imaging techniques and complex diagnoses, particularly in cardiology and oncology. Each category serves specific clinical requirements.</p></p>
