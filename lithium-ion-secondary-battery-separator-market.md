<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Secondary Battery Separator market is primarily segmented into bilayer and trilayer separators. Bilayer separators consist of two distinct layers, providing enhanced performance through improved mechanical strength and thermal stability. In contrast, trilayer separators feature three layers, which enhance ion transportation and minimize the risk of short circuits, ensuring higher safety and efficiency. Both types are crucial in optimizing battery performance for electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage applications, catering to varying industry needs.</p></p>
